Cox's Bazar, known for having the longest natural sea beach in the world, is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Bangladesh. Stretching over 120 kilometers, the beach is lined with golden sands and bordered by the deep blue waters of the Bay of Bengal. Visitors can enjoy swimming, surfing, and sunbathing, while exploring nearby spots like Himchari National Park and Inani Beach. Cox’s Bazar is also a gateway to Saint Martin’s Island, a serene coral island located off the southern coast.

With its picturesque landscape and relaxing atmosphere, Cox’s Bazar is perfect for both local and international tourists looking for a beach holiday. The nearby attractions offer a diverse experience for visitors, ensuring there’s something for everyone to enjoy. The bustling town surrounding the beach offers various shops, restaurants, and hotels, making it a comfortable spot for tourists to stay.
